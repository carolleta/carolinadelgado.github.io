Bio

Multinational Visual Artist living in London

 

Argentinian, Spanish, Venezuelan and Chilean nationality.

 

After my degree in Audio-Visual Design in the FADU, UBA in Buenos Aires Argentina in 2002, I decided to move to Madrid and work on the internet as a Web Designer.  In 2006 I moved to London and continue working as a Web Designer and more recently as a UX/UI Designer. In 2007 I started to study art a bit more seriously, reading theory, visiting art museums around the world, taking life drawing classes and later on oil painting classes with the old masters techniques.

 

In November 2015 I decided to go a bit further and rent an art studio. I started developing ideas for abstract art and my first attempt was 'Reconstructing Canvas'.  This study series tries to unveil the idea that reality can be changed, that it can be constructed again. Followed by 'Units', which is both Digital Art/New Media and Mixed Media exploration.
